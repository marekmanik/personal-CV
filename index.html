Marek Mánik is a PhD. candidate at the Thermophysical laboratory in the Faculty of Natural Sciences, Constantine the Philosopher university in Nitra. His disertation thesis is specialised on the mass transport of ionic solutions through the porous building materials with the properties of the semipermeable membranes. Except for the university, he is also a technician at the Building Testing and Research Institute, branch Nitra, where he performs the testing of building materials according to the European Technical Standards, mostly the thermal conductivity and/or resistance of thermal insulation materials.
